# Vidyarthi Digital Library – Website Project Documentation

## Overview

**Vidyarthi Digital Library** is a modern silent study zone designed for students, competitive exam aspirants, researchers, freelancers, and professionals seeking a distraction-free environment for focused work and learning.

Unlike traditional libraries, Vidyarthi Digital Library offers private study cabins equipped with essential amenities that help users maximize productivity, concentration, and comfort.

The website should communicate professionalism, calmness, productivity, privacy, and academic excellence.
